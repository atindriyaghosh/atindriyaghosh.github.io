About Me



As you probably would have guessed by now, my name is Atindriya Ghosh, Atin for short. I am a Java Developer currently employed at Accenture.

My work at Accenture primarily focuses on using Java in conjunction with Big Data technologies such as Hadoop, Cassandra, Hive etc. Much of what I have learnt in these (still) maturing technolgies is the result of reading many wonderful blog posts, comments etc. This website is mostly my attempt to share what I have learnt with all of you.

As it says on my home page, my focus is more on Big Data technologies. My attempt is to concentrate my efforts on developing various solutions using these technologies and writing about them.

That being said, I don't plan to restrict myself to just Big Data or Java. If I learn or read something interesting, I might write a post about it.

If you have read this far, I hope you have a general idea of what you can expect from this blog. I sincerely hope that you enjoy my articles and please do share your thoughts, questions and comments.
